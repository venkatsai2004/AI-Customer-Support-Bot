Resetting Your Password
To reset your password, go to the login page and click "Forgot Password." Enter your email address and follow the link sent to your email inbox. Make sure to check your spam folder if you don't see the email within 5 minutes.

Refund Policy
We offer full refunds within 30 days of purchase for any reason. To request a refund, contact our support team at support@example.com with your order number. Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days and will appear on your original payment method.

Contacting Support
Our support team is available to help you with any questions or issues. Email us at support@example.com or call our toll-free number 1-800-555-1234 during business hours (9 AM - 5 PM EST, Monday through Friday). For urgent issues, use the live chat feature on our website.

Account Management
You can update your account information by logging into your account dashboard. From there, you can change your email address, update billing information, and manage your subscription preferences. If you need to delete your account, please contact support.

Shipping Information
We offer free standard shipping on orders over $50. Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days, while express shipping (2-day delivery) is available for an additional fee. International shipping is available to most countries and takes 7-14 business days.

Technical Support
If you're experiencing technical issues with our software, first try clearing your browser cache and cookies. Make sure you're using a supported browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge). If problems persist, contact our technical support team with details about your operating system and browser version.

Privacy and Security
We take your privacy seriously and use industry-standard encryption to protect your data. We never share your personal information with third parties without your consent. You can review our complete Privacy Policy on our website under the Legal section.

Billing Questions
Billing questions can be resolved by contacting our billing department at billing@example.com. Common billing issues include updating payment methods, understanding charges, and managing subscription renewals. We accept all major credit cards and PayPal.
